Let's make open data so easy that everyone publishes and uses it

EasierOpenData.org is a brand new nonprofit organization founded by Tim Clemans of Seattle.
The mission: Make it insanely easy to publish and use open data.
The vision: All the world's public information will be freely available online in a standardized machine readable format that software make accesssible to humans through a very fast and powerful API.
